Diverse societysocial studies
features of a diverse society
  • Nationality
    Nationality Vietnam healthy low obesity vs USA unhealthy high obesity
    used to different history and conventions belonging to upbringing in respective countries → different principles → different attitudes and views on one idea → different identities in the same society → diversity
  • Race and ethnicity
    Race and ethnicity Chinese women wear Cheongsam, Indian women Saris
    Race: classification according to physical characteristics
    Ethnicity: ancestry, culture, language
    different cultural practices → varied choices depending on their ethnicities → distinct ways of lives → different identities in the same society → diversity
  • Religion
    Religion Muslim - halal, Hindus - no beef
    organised collection of attitudes, beliefs, practices with the worhipping of God → varied choices depending on their religion → distinct ways of lives → different identities in the same society → diversity
  • Socio-economic status
    Socio-economic status Cotton On vs Hermes
    economic situation shared by a group of people considering income, occupation, education, and wealth → different choice of housing, food, entertainment and activities based on SES → distinct ways of lives → different identities in the same society → diversity
